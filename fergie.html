Explored methods of translating through design, with consideration of the elastic nature of meaning.

Tasked to design a translation of Fergie’s wikipedia article assigned by random selection. The information in the article directed the focus on critiquing modern day pop music and how the lyrics are catchy but feels that there is a “lack of nutricion.”

The translation was executed through describing Fergie’s music as a convenient bag of chips, which is fast, cheap, savory, though does not contain many nutrients. Additionally, the overly colorful strips of music puts emphasis on the idea of artificiality and consumerism.
