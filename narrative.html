Tasked to create a spatial interpretation of a “narrative” using paper-cutting techniques. In this non-conventional book form, there are a total of 7 pages that collapse and open to reveal a sequence of two trains passing through an underground railway. Challenged the idea of what a narrative can be, and the intentions were to focus on the mundane sequences and narratives in our daily lives, such as riding a train.

Instructor: Gabriel Feld
